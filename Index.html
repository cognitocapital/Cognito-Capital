Tokenomics

Cognito Capital's tokenomics model is designed to foster a sustainable and potentially deflationary economic environment. The COG token has multiple utilities within the ecosystem.

Token Distribution

  • Community and Ecosystem: 45%
  • Team and Advisors: 15% (Vested)
  • Founder (DeepThought): 15% (Vested)
  • Treasury: 25% (Includes Bitcoin Reserve via Zeus Network, a16z Mirrored Portfolio, and DeFai Portfolio)

Deflationary Mechanism

A portion of the platform's revenue will be used for COG token buybacks from the open market, with a dynamic rate adjusted based on the performance of the Bitcoin reserve and the a16z/DeFai portfolios. A part of these repurchased tokens will be permanently burned.

Utility of COG

  • Governance: COG holders can vote on proposals affecting both the Cognito Capital platform and the Cognito Oracle network.
  • Staking: Users can stake COG to become node operators in the Cognito Oracle network or gain access to premium features of the investment platform.
  • Access to Cognito Oracle: DeFi protocols and users may need to hold or stake COG for access to premium data feeds or analyses.
  • Fee Discounts: Users holding or staking COG may receive discounts on platform fees.
